EMPYREAN

We are nothing but tiny specs of stardust in this immense universe.

Drifting away on our tiny rock. We are just lost in this expanse.

For the empyrean is so immense and the reaches so far beyond.

All we can do is watch it's glorius wonders.

After all, we are just specs of dust in this immense empyrean.

Too big to encompass.

Entire lifetimes prove inconsequential in front of the sheer size.

All the worlds are drifting around.

Each world has a story worth knowing.

Each with it's moments of sorrow and that of glee.
